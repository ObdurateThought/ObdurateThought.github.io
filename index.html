When learning to code, I thought it would be a good idea to have a goal in mind for me to drive towards. I decided to code a bot using Twitter API that would post cool and interesting images daily.

The posts are made by reading the top scores of a selection of subreddits via reddit and then taking the images and title and posting accordingly and then giving credit if certain parameters are met.

Through time, the account has undergone a few changes. This includes the connection between a list of keywords and emoji's as an article once suggested Tweets with emoji's get more interactions.

Takedown

Removing A Tweet

It's understandable that sometimes the bot will post an image from a photographer and it may not give proper credits. I've tried to do so as much as possible, although it's almost impossible to get it right 100% of the time. If you've fallen victim to this and would like the Tweet removed, send a Direct Message to @ObdurateThought and I'll get to it the second I see it.

Proper Credits

If you don't mind your image being on the account but the bot hasn't given proper credits, then send a Direct Message to @ObdurateThought and I'll be happy to remove the Tweet and resubmit with any and all credits along with any links you would like (So long as they are not malicious).
